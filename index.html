About

I'm a network engineer who splits time between two worlds that don't usually share an org chart: wireless RF design and service-provider routing. That combination means I think about a network end to end — from the antenna pattern on an access point to the BGP policy that decides which path a packet takes out to the internet.

Most of my work has centered on high-density wireless deployments (stadiums, campuses, warehouses) and multi-site MPLS/SD-WAN builds for carriers and their enterprise customers. I care about the details that show up at 2 a.m. — co-channel interference, route flapping, a QoS policy that looked fine on paper.

Based in Manila, PH, working with teams across APAC and remote-first globally.
